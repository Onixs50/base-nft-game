Game Info

Mint NFTs by connecting your wallet and clicking the "Mint NFT" button. Each NFT has a different probability:

  • NFT #3: 30%
  • NFT #1: 25%
  • NFT #2: 25%
  • NFT #4: 20%

Once you have all 4 NFTs, you can mint the rare 5th NFT!
